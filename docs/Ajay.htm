GEMZ will be the broker between the jewelry designer/seller and the buyer.  The designer/seller can upload jewelry designs and add to their profile/bio/profile picture (http://www.saatchiart.com/michaltrpak) after approval from GEMZ administrator.  ALL purchasing transactions will go through GEMZ.  GEMZ will handle payment from the buyer and then payout to designer/seller (via sales/payout page – see http://www.saatchiart.com/accounts/items).
